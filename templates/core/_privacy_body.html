Privacy notice

This is a private coaching portal. Your account is created by an administrator and your data is visible only to you, your assigned coach (while the coaching relationship is active), and the site administrator.

  • Your profile, measurements, workouts, nutrition targets, supplement recommendations, and uploaded files are private to you and your coach.
  • Uploaded files are stored privately and are never publicly accessible.
  • You can download a copy of your data at any time from Account Settings.
  • You can deactivate your account yourself; permanent deletion is handled by the administrator on request.
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Questions? Contact your administrator.
